Privacy Policy for "Coupons Finder"

Last updated: [Insert Date]

The "Coupons Finder" extension is designed to help users find Coupons while shopping online.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect or store any personal data from users.

The only information accessed by the extension is the URL of the current tab you are browsing, used to identify applicable coupons for the website.

Data Usage

The current tab URL is temporarily accessed to fetch relevant coupons in real time. No data is stored, logged, or transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with external parties. All operations occur locally within your browser.
